FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 8-16 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. I'm fluent in English and Japanese, and I'm comfortable working across different time zones with flexible scheduling.

What's included in your development process?

My process includes initial consultation, wireframing, design mockups, development, testing, deployment, and 30 days of post-launch support. I also provide documentation and training for your team.

Can you maintain and update existing projects?

Yes, I can work on existing codebases, perform updates, add new features, and optimize performance. I also offer ongoing maintenance contracts for long-term support.
